Go Fish Rules

How to Play Go Fish

  1. The game is played with 2-6 players.
  2. Each player is dealt 7 cards (or 5 if there are more than 3 players).
  3. The remaining cards are placed face down to form a draw pile.
  4. Players take turns asking other players for cards to make sets of four.
  5. If the asked player has the requested card, they must give it up.
  6. If not, they say "Go fish," and the asker draws from the pile.
  7. When a player collects a set of four, they place it face up in front of them.
  8. The game ends when all sets have been collected.
  9. The player with the most sets wins!
